# Changes in GST and Cess Rates (Effective September 22, 2025)

The tax structure was simplified by moving many items from a 28% GST plus a variable cess to an all-inclusive 18% or 40% GST rate.

| Category of Goods | Old GST Rate + Cess (Before Sept 22, 2025) | New GST Rate (Inclusive of Cess) |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Luxury Cars** | 28% GST + up to 22% Cess (up to 50% total) | **40% GST** |
| **Aerated Drinks** | 28% GST + 12% Cess (40% total) | **40% GST** |

| **Small Cars (Petrol)** | 28% GST + 1% Cess (29% total) | **18% GST** (Cess removed) |
| **Premium Motorcycles (>350cc)** | 28% GST + up to 15% Cess (up to 43% total) | **40% GST** |
| **Tobacco Products** | 28% GST + Variable Cess | **28% GST + Variable Cess** (No change) |
